Services

Design-Bid-Build

This is the traditional method where the project is divided into distinct phases. The owner first hires an architect to create complete, detailed designs.

Design-Build

In this model, the design and construction services are bundled into a single contract. The owner works with one entity responsible for both design and construction.

Construction Management at Risk

Under this service, a manager acts as a consultant during the design phase and sets a guaranteed maximum price for the project.
